# License and Citation

This work, Kivy: Deep Recipe Embeddings for Generation, Retrieval, and Nutritional Analysis, is released freely for academic, research, and non-commercial use. Our aim is to advance the field of food computing by providing open access to a high-quality dataset and a reproducible recipe embedding model.

## Citation

You are welcome to use, adapt, and build upon the dataset, model architecture, and methods described in this document, provided that proper credit is given.
